表达不喜欢的词语

hate, cannot stand, cannot bear.

**loathe**: to hate someone or something very much. eg I absolutely loathe shopping.

**detest**: to hate someone or something very much. eg The two men detested each other.

**despise**: to dislike and have a low opinion of someone or something. eg She despised her neighbours.
