What is the role of time? ... Time prevents everything from being given at once ... It is not the vehicle of creativity and choice? Is not the existence of time the proof of indeterminism in nature?

——*The Possible and the Real* by Bergson

Human existence consists of 'constantly creating and unpredictable new things'. Time has proven that there is uncertainty in nature. The universe around us is just one of many 'possible' worlds.
