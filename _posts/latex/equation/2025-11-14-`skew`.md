```latex
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{amsmath}

\newcommand{\skewhat}[1]{\skew{2}\hat{#1}}

\begin{document}

$$\ddot{h} \iff \skew{-2}{\ddot}{h}$$

$$\hat{AB} \iff \skewhat{AB}$$

\end{document}
```
