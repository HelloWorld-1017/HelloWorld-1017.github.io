**sic**(sic erat scriptum): used after a word that you have copied in order to show that you know it was not spelled or used correctly.

**i.e.** : written before a word or phrase that gives the exact meaning of something you have just written or said.

**e.g.** (Latin exempli gratia “for the sake of example”) : the abbreviation of FOR EXAMPLE, used when you are listing some examples of things you are talking about.
