# 后缀(suffix)

## 名词后缀

### -er/-or

用来表示进行某种活动的人，比如：writer, painter, worker, shopper, teacher, lawer.

cricketer(cricket): someone who plays cricket.

stockbroker(stock)：a person or organization whose jobis to buy and sell shares, bonds etc for people.

在许多动词后面可以加上 -er，变成名词。

maker: showmaker, sorrymaker 😂

有时，-er 后缀也可以写作 -or。这类单词有：actor, operator, sailor, supervisor.

-er/-or 也可以用来表示有某种特定功能的物体，比如：pencil-sharpener, projector.

**bottle-opener**: a small tool used for removing the metal lids from bottles.

**grater**: a tool used for grating food.

grate: to rub cheese, vegetables etc against a rough or sharp surface in order to break them into small pieces.
{: .notice--primary}

---

### -er , -ee

-er 和 -ee 可以用来表示两种相对的意思，-er 表示“做某事的人”，而 -ee 则表示“接收或经历该行为的人”。如：employer/employee, sender/addressee, payee.

**addressee**: the person a letter, package etc is addressed to.
{: .notice--primary}

**payee**: the person or organization to whom money, especially a cheque, must be paid.
{: .notice--primary}

---

### -(t)ion/-sion/-ion

-(t)ion/-sion/-ion 用来把动词变为名词。例如，pollution(pollute)，alteration(alter), donation(donate).

**complication**(complicate): a problem or situation that makes something more difficult to understand or deal with. 
e.g. The fact that the plane was late added a further complication to our journey.

 **reduction**(reduce): a decrease in the size, price, or amount of something, or the act of decreasing something.

**promotion**(promote): a move to a more important job or position in a company or organization.

**admission**(admit): a statement in which you admit that something is true or that you have done something wrong.

**obsession**(obsess): an extreme unhealthy interest in something or worry about something, which stops you from thinking about anything else, eg *Prime Obsession*.
